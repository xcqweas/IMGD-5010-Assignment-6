## Variations

This now includes random factors and the overall population to influence whether the cell will die or live (basic conditions not changed). Not very balanced.

### The Influence

#### Factor 1: Randomness

Just a series of random numbers used to determine.

#### Factor 2: World Population

Generally speaking, the more live cells there are, the more likely they will die and less likely to give birth, otherwise less likely to die and more likely to give birth. On top of these, there's a low chance "repopulation" that will only activate when the number of living cells is very low.

#### Factor 3: Survival Difficulty

A float. Higher value makes dying more likely and birth less likely, vice versa. The most unbalanced value here.
